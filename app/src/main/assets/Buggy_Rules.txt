BUGGY RULES

1. A game of bug is initiated by  the announcing of Bug or Buggy,
   any one who wishes to play may, but is responsible for keeping
   their own score.(Now thanks to the Buggy app scores are logged-with
   with picture proof of "buggy's".)

2. Buggy's are Volkswagen beetle cars that are found driving around on the
   road approximately from 1964-2022 models all beetles are subject to the
   game of buggy.

3. Car Lots with Buggy's are Exempt from counting.

4. 8 Bug's initiates a Slug Bug The slug bug score wins the round, all scores
   reset to zero the slug bug score is tallied as a score in the winners
   circle for high score record.
